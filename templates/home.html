Check My Firewall


FWCheck is a firewall rule analyzer that helps you ensure your firewall rules are sane before you put them in production. To use FWCheck, upload your configuration file using the form below and click the "Get Report" button FWCheck will generate a report that tells you which rules, if any, should be modified. Once you are finished, click the "Finished" button and your uploaded configuration file and report will be removed from our server. All uploaded files and reports are removed after 24 hours.
